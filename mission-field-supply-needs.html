Mission & Field Supply Needs

CPI specializes in the receipt and distribution of donated sterile medical disposable supplies and materials. In general, we do not accept medical equipment for distribution in the U.S. or overseas.
Because we have limited space in our warehouse, and we desire to be good stewards of all the resources the Lord has provided to us, we are very specific about the medical supplies we receive as donations. Please, if you have any questions regarding our donation acceptance policies, or if you are unsure if your donation meets our standards, we would ask you to contact our office directly at 937-743-2744 before bringing your supply donations to our warehouse.
Below is a listing of the medical supplies we accept.
Please note, if any product listed below has been used, has been opened or damaged, or is not in its original condition, we cannot accept those products.

What we can accept

  • OB/GYN Supplies
  • Orthopedic Supplies  (casting material, immobilizers, supports, etc.)
  • Ostomy Supplies
  • Pediatric and NICU Supplies
  • Respiratory Supplies  (nasal cannulas, oxygen masks, etc.)
  • Sponges - Foam, Lap Pads, X-Ray Detectable
  • Suction Supplies - Catheters, Kits, Tubing,  Yankauers/Frazier Tips
  • Surgical Linens - Cloth/Paper, Sterile  (drapes, sheets, towels, etc.) (Accepted from Healthcare facilities only.)
  • Sterile Surgical Procedure Packs
  • Sutures
  • Syringes - Bulb Syringes, Irrigation, Oral, Traditional (all sizes)
  • Tape - All types (Durapore, Medipore, self-sticking, Transpore, etc.)
  • Tracheostomy Tubes and Care Supplies
  • Underpads/Chux - Cloth or Disposable
  • Urinary Drainage - Bags, Catheters (Foley, Straight, Texas), Insertion Kits
  • Ace Bandages
  • Alcohol Prep Pads and Swabsticks
  • Anesthesia Supplies
  • Sterile Scalpels
  • Betadine or Povidone-Iodine Ointment, Pads, Solution, and Swabsticks
  • Compression Stockings - Ted, Jobst
  • Diapers - Adult & Pediatric
  • Drains - Chest Drains, Penrose, Jackson-Pratt, etc.
  • Dressings - Sterile or Non-Sterile  (Coban, Duoderm, Gauze, Xeroform, etc.)
  • EKG Electrodes, Sterile
  • Exam Supplies (cotton balls, Q-tips, thermometers, tongue depressors, etc.)
  • IV Solution - Dextrose ≤5%, Saline (bottles, unused IV, jars; NO FLUSHES)
  • Gloves - Sterile or Non-Sterile
  • Gowns - Sterile or Non-Sterile
  • Head and Shoe Covers, Goggles, Masks
  • Hospital Linens - Blankets, Pillows, Sheets (Accepted from Healthcare facilities only.)
  • IV Supplies
  • LMA’s, Endotracheal Tubes
  • Needles - Angio-Catheters, Blood Draw, Butterfly, Port Access, Spinal, Traditional

What We Cannot Accept

  • C-Pap Machines or any accessories such as masks, tubing, etc.
  • Custom-fitted (formed) wheelchairs
  • Used orthopedic braces, ace wraps, etc.
  • Pill bottles or any medication pre-prescribed to an individual
  • Feeding supplies or tubing
  • Items that are or appear to be hazardous or dangerous.
  • Open packs of diapers or bed pads or feminine products
  • Commodes/Potty Chairs/Shower Chairs not in their original box or packaging
  • Clothes of any kind
  • Liquids of any kind
  • Glucometers without Test Strips or Expired Test Strips

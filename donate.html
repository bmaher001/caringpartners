Donor Advised Fund

You can support Caring Partners International through a Donor-Advised Fund (DAF). A DAF allows you to make a charitable contribution, receive an immediate tax benefit, and then recommend grants to CPI over time. As a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit, CPI is eligible to receive DAF contributions, making it simple and flexible for you to direct your support where it's needed most.

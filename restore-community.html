Restore Community

Christ tells us to love one another as ourselves. Restore Community invites Tri-State families, organizations, and businesses to join CPI in assembling Care Bags and Care Packs for those in need within our local community. Our church partners deliver these donations, ensuring physical and spiritual needs are met.

Caring Partners International invites Tri-State corporations to join our Restore Community outreach initiative, which combines corporate team building with meaningful community service. This program empowers employees to actively participate in serving their local communities by assembling personal care supplies for men, women, and children in need.

Cost-Effective Impact

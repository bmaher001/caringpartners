Get Involved

Volunteers are essential to the success of Caring Partners’ ministries. Every year, our dedicated volunteers contribute over 5,000 hours to support our "Evangelism Through Medicine®" initiatives. We sincerely appreciate the hundreds of volunteers who generously give their time and effort to further the Kingdom of God.

Volunteer Opportunities Include

  • Office Support
  • Landscaping & Outdoor Maintenance
  • Care Package Assembly
  • Medical Product Evaluation & Sorting
  • Non-Medical Product Sorting
  • Receiving, Sorting, and Boxing Supplies
  • Warehouse, Office, and Building Maintenance
  • And many other fulfilling roles . . .

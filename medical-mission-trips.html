Medical Mission Trips

Caring Partners International (CPI) organizes medical mission trips that fill up quickly, providing volunteers with opportunities to serve in locations worldwide.

These trips enable licensed healthcare professionals to treat patients, deliver medical supplies, and train local healthcare providers while equipping local churches to share the Gospel of Jesus Christ. Non-medical team members can also be involved in our healthcare clinics.
To participate, you must apply, and receive approval. Once you are approved, a deposit is required. If a trip is listed as FULL, do not apply unless you have received per-approval from a Team Leader.

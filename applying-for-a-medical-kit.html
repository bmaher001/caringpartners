Applying for a Medical Kit

Follow our simple 2-step process to request medical supplies for your mission trip.

⚠️

Important Timing Information

Please submit your Medical Kit Request at least SIX WEEKS prior to your Mission Trip. A representative from your Church must come to the Caring Partners' Warehouse to pick up your Medical Kit, or shipping arrangements can be made (recipient responsible for shipping costs).

Complete These Steps

1

Medical Kit Request

Complete our online request form with your church information, mission details, and the medical supplies you need. The form includes a comprehensive pack list where you can specify quantities for each item.

Start Request Form →
2

Follow-up Report

Within THREE WEEKS after your Mission Trip, please complete the Follow-up Report to share how the medical supplies were used and the impact of your mission work.

Submit Follow-up Report →

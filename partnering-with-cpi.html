Partnering with CPI

Caring Partners International (CPI) is a Christian ministry committed to equipping Bible-believing churches to address both the physical and spiritual healthcare needs of communities worldwide. By fulfilling these essential needs, we create opportunities for churches to share the transformative message of Jesus Christ.

How CPI Equips Local Churches

‍

  • Medical Mission Trips:
    • At the invitation of local churches, pastors, and missionaries, CPI dispatches short-term medical teams globally. These teams conduct healthcare clinics, often hosted in church facilities. At the same time, local partners concurrently hold evangelistic services—this dual approach results in numerous individuals receiving both physical care and spiritual salvation through Christ.
  • Medical Supplies for Missions:
    • Operating from our 8,000-square-foot warehouse in Franklin, Ohio, CPI collects and prepares millions of dollars’ worth of medical supplies. Our dedicated volunteers ensure that these essential resources reach church-sponsored projects globally and domestically, providing vulnerable individuals with crucial healthcare items such as hospital beds, wheelchairs, and crutches.
  • Tri-State Care Network:
    • Through this initiative, CPI distributes home healthcare supplies and personal hygiene products to church partners, enabling them to effectively address both the physical and spiritual needs of their communities.

Ways Your Church Can Get Involved

‍

  • Organize or join a volunteer medical mission team to support global missionary work.
  • Volunteer at our Franklin, Ohio, facility, contributing to mission-critical projects that save lives physically and spiritually.
  • Join our Tri-State Care Network to distribute essential products to those in need within your local area (Northern Kentucky, Eastern Indiana, Greater Cincinnati, and Dayton).
  • Financially partner with CPI through your church’s mission budgets or Faith Promise campaigns.
  • Invite CPI missionaries to your church events to share insights and experiences from our medical ministries and “Evangelism Through Medicine®” programs.

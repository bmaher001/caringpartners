Belize 2026 
Medical Mission Trip

January 28 – February 4, 2026

Vision & Purpose

Caring Partners International seeks to bring both physical healing and spiritual hope to the people of Belize. This mission trip provides an opportunity for medical professionals and volunteers to serve communities with limited access to healthcare offering free medical services, education, and compassionate care. Through these acts of service, our team shares the love of Christ and helps local churches continue their ministry long after the trip has ended.

Can you provide the required documents

Required Documents

‍

  • Completed online application form.
  • Valid passport (at least 6 months past the travel date).
  • For minors under 18: Pre-approval by a Team Leader is required. Notarized parental consent letter is needed if not traveling with both parents.
  • Any required vaccination or medical certificates for entry (you will be informed of any needed details).
  • Pastor/Spiritual Leader Information (name, church title, email, phone).
  • Confidential Church Recommendation:
    • If this is your first time serving with CPI:
      • When filling out the application, you will provide your pastor or spiritual leader’s contact information.
      • CPI will email your pastor a confidential recommendation link (form), which he will complete privately. This is a required step for first-time participants.
    • If you have served on a CPI trip before:
      • You do not need to submit a new church recommendation.

Required Documents

‍

  1. Pray for God’s guidance.
  2. Submit your online application, including all required documents and, if it’s your first trip, your pastor’s contact details: [Apply Online]
  3. Confirmation of Receipt:
    • Immediately after submitting your application and uploading all documents, you will receive a confirmation message on the website and via email stating that your application has been received and is under review.
  4. Waiting Period:
    • Your application will be reviewed within up to 10 business days.
    • During this time, the mission team or trip leader may contact you for clarification if information is missing, incorrect, or unclear.
  5. Decision Notification:
    • You will receive an official acceptance or regret email within 10 days.
    • If accepted:
      • You will receive a congratulatory email, plus a direct link to pay your deposit ($500) and instructions for the final payment deadline.
      • You’ll receive a Key Dates schedule addressing meeting dates and payment deadlines.
    • If not accepted:
      • You will receive a regret email (and, if possible, a brief explanation).

Please check your email regularly during this process and make sure your contact information is accurate.

FAQs

What about visas or entry paperwork?

You’ll be informed in advance if there are any special visa requirements for your chosen country of travel.

Will there be a pre-trip orientation?

Two team meetings will be scheduled before departure and it is mandatory for each team member to attend at least one of these meetings, in-person, or via Google Meet.

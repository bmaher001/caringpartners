Our Programs

At Caring Partners International, we strive to bring hope and healing to those in need, physically and spiritually. Through our core programs, we partner with churches, organizations, companies, and individuals to deliver medical care, essential supplies, and spiritual outreach. Every initiative is designed to equip churches, support the vulnerable, and make a lasting difference—locally and globally. Explore our programs below to see how we’re serving together.

Medical Mission Trips

Caring Partners International mobilizes skilled medical teams through our Medical Mission Trips to serve in areas where healthcare is scarce or unavailable, such as Guatemala, Belize, and Thailand. We work closely alongside local churches to deliver essential medical care, support community health, and open doors for spiritual outreach. Each mission is a testament to our commitment: providing practical help, compassionate encouragement, and lasting impact in places that need it most.

Request Medical Supplies  for Missions

CPI is committed to ensuring that life-saving resources reach those who need them most. Our Medical Supplies for Missions program coordinates the collection, preparation, and worldwide distribution of high-quality medical supplies, working in close partnership with local churches that serve as vital bridges between our teams and communities in need. We empower clinics, hospitals, and mission teams to deliver effective care and restore hope in underserved regions by bridging the gap between surplus and shortage.

Tri-State Care Network Program

Caring Partners International (CPI) supports the Tri-State Care Network—a coalition of local churches and faith-based organizations—by providing essential health and hygiene supplies to those in need. While CPI does not directly serve clients, it equips local partners to distribute items like home health aids, hygiene materials, baby care supplies, and nutritional products. Relying on community volunteers and its 20 years of supply-chain experience, CPI helps meet both the physical and spiritual needs of underserved families in Southwest Ohio through local churches.

Restore Community

Christ tells us to love one another as ourselves. Restore Community welcomes individuals, families, and businesses to join CPI in assembling Care Bags and Care Packs for those facing hardships within our communities. Our local church partners deliver the donations, ensuring physical and spiritual needs are met.

Schools, Organizations, and Businesses are welcome.
